# Competitor Mapping Skill

This skill enables the agent to scan the competitive landscape and identify startup rivals.

## Capabilities
1. **Competitor Discovery**: Identifies direct and indirect competitors based on the company's product description.
2. **Pricing & Feature Extraction**: Details competitors' pricing structures and product details.
3. **Market Density Verdict**: Evaluates if the market is crowded or open.

## Usage
Invoke the `search_competitors` tool with:
- `company_name` (string)
- `description` (string)
